Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (commonly known as CS ) is a highly popular first-person shooter (FPS) game developed by Valve Corporation and Hidden Path Entertainment. It was released in 2012 as the fourth game in the Counter-Strike series. Here's an overview of what makes CS unique:

Gemeplay

  • Teams: The game pits two teams against each other: Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists. Each team has different objectives depending on the game mode.
  • Objective: In the most popular game mode, Bomb Defusal, the Terrorists aim to plant a bomb at specific bomb sites, while the Counter-Terrorists must prevent this or defuse the bomb if it has been planted. Another common mode is Hostage Rescue, where Counter-Terrorists try to rescue hostages held by the Terrorists.
  • Rounds: The game is divided into rounds, and the first team to win a set number of rounds (usually 16 in competitive play) wins the match.
  • Economy System: Players earn in-game money by winning rounds, completing objectives, or getting kills. This money is used to buy better weapons, armor, and utility items like grenades for the next round.

  4. Free-to-Play: Dota 2 is free to download and play, but offers cosmetic items and skins for purchase, which do not affect gameplay.
